Apeparty Rules

  • Players of all ages are welcome, but please avoid disruptive or inappropriate behavior.
  • Discrimination of any kind is not allowed. This includes but is not limited to racism, sexism, homophobia, and ableism.
  • Be respectful of others and avoid saying or doing anything that may make other players feel uncomfortable or unsafe. This includes but is not limited to harassment, bullying, and hate speech.
  • Use appropriate language and keep conversations civil and respectful.
  • Do not share personal information, such as your real name, address, or phone number, with other players.
  • Do not engage in cheating or exploit any bugs or glitches in the game.
  • Do not impersonate or falsely represent yourself as another player or member of the game's staff.
  • Follow the instructions of the game's admins and staff and do not engage in behavior that disrupts gameplay.
  • Do not use any external software or hardware that modifies the game's code or gives you an unfair advantage.
  • Report any suspicious or inappropriate behavior to the game's staff.
